About

The Trimurti Group of Educational Institutes operates 14 schools and 11 colleges across Ahmednagar and Amravati districts of Maharashtra. Our institutions offer world-class academic curricula in CBSE and state board (English, Semi & Marathi medium) as well as junior college, D.Ed college, B.Ed college, home science, and B.Sc college programs in accordance with university standards. At the school level, our institutions adopt a military-style pattern similar to Sainik Schools, which includes basic military training for all cadets. We are committed to providing a holistic education that combines academic excellence with military discipline.

Mission

M

Our school is dedicated to helping cadets become responsible citizens of their community, nation, and the world. We provide rigorous instruction in basic skills and preparation to ensure that our students are well-prepared for success. To be effective, we believe that education must be reinforced with order, structure, and discipline. Our goal is to foster the spiritual, social, emotional, physical, and intellectual growth of our students within a healthy, wholesome environment.

Vision

V

Trimurti Pawan Pratishthan’s vision is to be the top military preparatory school for installing tradition, knowledge and leadership, enabling students to achieve their full potential.
