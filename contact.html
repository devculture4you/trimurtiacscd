About US

The Trimurti Group of Educational Institutes operates 14 schools and 11 colleges across Ahmednagar and Amravati districts of Maharashtra. Our institutions offer world-class academic curricula in CBSE and state board (English, Semi & Marathi medium) as well as junior college, D.Ed college, B.Ed college, home science, and B.Sc college programs in accordance with university standards. At the school level, our institutions adopt a military-style pattern similar to Sainik Schools, which includes basic military training for all cadets. We are committed to providing a holistic education that combines academic excellence with military discipline.
